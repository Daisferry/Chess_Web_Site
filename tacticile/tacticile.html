Forking Forking is when a piece simultaneously attacks 2 pieces at once while being either defended or unable to be captured.
Pinning Pinning is when a lower value piece is protecting a heigher value piece, resulting in the piece being unable or unfavourable to move.
Skewers Skewers are when a high value piece is protecting a low value piece that isn't defended from enemy attacks resulting in the piece needing to be moved away and leaving the low value peice to be taken.
Discovered Attacks Discovered attacks happen when a piece move to reveal a check while also attacking another piece, guaranteeing value
Trapping Pieces Trapping a piece happens when a piece is attack and has no safe squares to move to, leading to it's inevitable capture and desperado.
Double Checks Double checks occur when a piece moves to check the king while also revealing another check, the only response to that type of attack is moving the king.
